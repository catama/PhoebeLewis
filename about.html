Getting there:

The Greenhouses are located within Brockwell Park in Lambeth. The site is in the middle of the park between the Walled Garden and the Tennis Courts.

The nearest train station is Herne Hill, the closest tube is Brixton (Victoria Line). Buses 3, 196, and 322 run from outside Brixton station to the corner of the park..

There are bike racks at the Greenhouses.
